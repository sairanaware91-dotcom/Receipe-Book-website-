Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate
  • Butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • Vanilla essence

Preparation Steps

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  2. In another bowl beat eggs and sugar.
  3. Mix melted chocolate with egg mixture.
  4. Add flour and vanilla essence.
  5. Pour batter into baking mould.
  6. Bake for 10–12 minutes.
  7. Serve warm with ice cream.
