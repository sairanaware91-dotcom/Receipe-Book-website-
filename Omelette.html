Omelette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter
  • Chopped onion
  • Chopped tomato

Preparation Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl.
  2. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Heat butter in a pan.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  5. Add onion and tomato on top.
  6. Cook for 2–3 minutes and fold.
