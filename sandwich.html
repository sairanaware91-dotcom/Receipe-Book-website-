Veg Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • Cucumber slices
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Spread butter evenly on both bread slices.
  2. Add tomato and cucumber slices.
  3. Place cheese on top of the vegetables.
  4. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper.
  5. Close the sandwich and cut into halves.
  6. Serve fresh with ketchup or chutney.
