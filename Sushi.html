Sushi Recipe

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets (seaweed)
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Carrot
  • Soy sauce

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook sushi rice and let it cool.
  2. Place nori sheet on a bamboo mat.
  3. Spread rice evenly on the sheet.
  4. Add cucumber, carrot and avocado.
  5. Roll tightly using the mat.
  6. Cut into small pieces and serve with soy sauce.
