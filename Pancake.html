Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Butter

Preparation Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar and baking powder.
  2. Add egg and milk.
  3. Mix well to make smooth batter.
  4. Heat butter in a pan.
  5. Pour batter and cook both sides.
  6. Serve with honey or syrup.
